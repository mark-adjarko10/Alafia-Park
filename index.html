About Us

Alafia Park has been in operation for the past 2 decades. Over the period, we have received quite a number of visitors who want to relax, destress, catch some breeze and escape from the world. Visitors' favourite spot is the riverside where they sit under the beautiful mango tree with their kids, a loved one or family to have some amazing,quality and beautiful time together.
